# BASS-HI: The Largest HI Imaging Survey of Hard X-ray Selected AGNs

**Unveiling the "Fueling and Feedback" Cycle: How Galaxies Assemble and How AGNs Regulate Star Formation.**

---

## ðŸŒŒ Overview
The **BASS-HI Survey** is a comprehensive HI imaging program dedicated to mapping the cold neutral hydrogen (HI) gas in AGN host galaxies. By leveraging the **Swift-BAT AGN Spectroscopic Survey (BASS)**, which selects AGNs using hard X-rays (14â€“195 keV), we bypass dust obscuration and star formation contamination. 

Our mission is to resolve the spatial and kinematic distribution of the cold gas reservoir to understand how supermassive black holes (SMBHs) are fueled and how their feedback impacts the evolution of their host galaxies.

---

## ðŸŽ¯ Key Science Goals

### 1. Resolving the AGN-HI Connection
We aim to settle the debate on whether AGN host galaxies are gas-deficient. By providing the first large-scale resolved imaging of a hard X-ray selected sample, we connect central black hole properties (mass, accretion rate) to the spatially-resolved HI morphology.

### 2. Tracing Gas Accretion Histories
Using the high sensitivity of the **VLA D-array**, we detect diffuse, extended HI features (bridges, streams, and tails) that are invisible in shallower surveys. These are the "smoking guns" of recent or ongoing gas accretion from the environment.

### 3. Pinpointing Quenching and Feedback
We investigate whether AGN activity quenches star formation by removing gas or triggers it through mechanical energy injection. By combining HI maps with multi-wavelength ancillary data (CO, IR, Optical), we build a complete picture of the "Galactic Ecosystem."

## ðŸ“¡ Observation Strategy
We utilize a multi-scale approach by combining different VLA configurations to capture both the "Detailed" and the "Diffuse."



| Configuration | Focus | Key Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **VLA C-array** | **Detailed Structure** | Resolving inner disk kinematics, gas stability, and localized outflows/jets. |
| **VLA D-array** | **Diffuse Reservoir** | Detecting extended structures and gas accretion from the IGM ($N_{HI} \approx 5 \times 10^{18} \text{ cm}^{-2}$). |
| **Integrated** | **Global Census** | Combining Northern (VLA/WSRT/GMRT) and Southern (ATCA) data for ~100 targets. |

## ðŸ’¡ Why Hard X-ray (Swift-BAT) Selection?
Unlike optical or soft X-ray surveys, the BASS selection provides:
* **Completeness:** Includes heavily obscured (Compton-thick) AGNs.
* **Unbiased Census:** Minimizes contamination from host galaxy star formation.
* **Direct Connection:** Probes the emission from the innermost regions near the central black hole.
